Playing music in our server is a privilege; if you abuse it, you get it taken away. Below are the guidelines for playing music in our server. Abide by these rules and you get to keep your privilege.

  1. Music must be played using an external program such as HLDJ or HLSS. Simply holding your open mic to your speakers will get you muted.
  2. If there are other players wanting to play music, you must take turns.
    • You get 1 minute after another player is done playing to play your song or you lose your turn.
    • Playing out of your turn will get you muted.
  3. Only songs are to be played. Sounds/sound effects, voices, or anything NOT a song will get you muted.
  4. Songs cannot be more than 10 minutes. Exceeding this limit will get you muted.
  5. Insure your songs are properly converted and are at a comfortable volume.
    • Songs that are too loud or that are crackly will get you muted and lose your turn.
  6. Playing the same song over and over again will get you muted and you will lose your turn.
    • Playing the same song twice in the same map (or within 45 minutes) counts as repeated playing. Please allow 2 full maps after playing a song before playing it again.
  7. Songs that simply loop a single part over and over, back to back, may only be played for 3 minutes at least before it will be considered repetitive.
  8. Be weary of the server time (top right corner of scoreboard). If the map changes during your song then you lose your turn.
  9. Just because you muted another music player does not mean you can play during their turn.
  10. Players wanting to talk on mic are allowed to speak over the music you play so don't get mad at them.
Players can and will be susceptible to punishments from infractions not listed in the above rules.

Repeat offenders will be PERMANENTLY MUTED.
